Privacy Policy 

This policy describes the ways we collect, store, use and protects your personal information. You accepted this policy when you signed up for our Service. We may amend this policy at any time by posting a revised version on our website. The revised version will be effective at the time we post it. In addition, if the revised version includes a substantial change, we will provide you with 30 days’ prior notice by posting notice of the change on SIRENGHANA website.
Throughout this policy, we use the term “Personal Information” to describe information that can be associated with a specific person and can be used to identify that person. We do not consider personal information to include information that has been anonymous so that it does not identify a specific user.

1. Personal Information Collection

1.1. When you visit the SIRENGHANA website, we collect your IP address and standard weblog information, such as your browser type and the pages you accessed on our website. 1.2. If you register or subscribe to services on our website, we collect the following types of information from you:

  • Contact information - your name, address, phone, email and other similar information.
  • Registration information - your first name, last name, username, password and other similar information.
  • You post your business on our website we collect information about your company.
1.3 Before permitting you to use our Services, we may require you to provide these information to us for the sole purpose of content management as enable you access and control the information you post on the website.
1.4. Finally, we may collect information about your computer or other access device for fraud prevention purposes.

2. Cookies

2.1. When you access our website, we, or our entity will track how our website is used, may place small data files called "cookies" on your computer.

2.2. We send a "session cookie" to your computer when you log in to your account. This type of cookie helps us to recognize you if you visit multiple pages on our site during the same session, so that we don’t need to ask you for your password on each page. Once you log out or close your browser, this cookie expires and no longer has any effect.

2.3. We also use longer-lasting cookies for other purposes such as to display your username and other data on your membership, so that you don't need to retype the username each time you log in to your account.

2.4. We encode our cookies so that only we can interpret the information stored in them. You are free to decline our cookies if your browser permits, but doing so may interfere with your use of our website. We may also collect information about your computer or other access device to mitigate risk and for fraud prevention purposes.

3. Personal Information Protection

3.1. We store and process your personal information on extremely secured servers. Our internet service providers are up to the task 24/7 to protect all information stored on their server.

4. Use of Personal Information

4.1. Our primary purpose in collecting personal information is to provide you with a secure, smooth, well-organized, and personalized experience. We may use your personal information to:

  • provide the services and customer support you request;
  • to enable you post information such as News, Jobs, comments etc. on our website;
  • prevent potentially prohibited or illegal activities, and enforce our Terms of Service;
  • organize, measure, and improve our services and the content and layout of our website;
  • send you targeted marketing, job information, newsletters, service update notices, and promotional offers based on your communication preferences

5. Accessing and Changing your Personal Information

You can review and edit your personal information at any time by logging in to your account and clicking the My Profile link.
